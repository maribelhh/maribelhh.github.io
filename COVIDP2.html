Distribution of the Average Daily Deaths per Million Reported by North America Countries During 2021 Grouped by GDP Percentile

Comparison of North American countries distribution of daily new deaths per million based on GDP percentile and percentage 65+years population, during 2021. The message is that countries with greater GDP reported more deaths per million, and that GDP is related to percentage of 65+ aged population.

Data Source: https://covid.ourworldindata.org/data/owid-covid-data.csv accessed 2/22/2023.
